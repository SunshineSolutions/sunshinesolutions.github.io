Welcome to Sunshine Solutions!

We foresee a fundamental shift in retail in the coming 5 years. As small and medium manufacturers and brands realize they can cut out their wholesale partners and sell themselves exclusively on the online marketplaces and drastically increase profit margins. We have witnessed Amazon implement changes annually that continue to make life difficult for traditional retailers, meanwhile Amazon continues to improve the process for brands and manufacturers to sell themselves. Our goal is to utilize our experience to teach companies the in’s and out of selling on the greater Marketplace so they can thrive as they transition into a complete vertically integrated organization.

Additionally, we have developed a “push button” application that integrates with Amazon and other marketplaces that schedules and stores seller data that can be used for:

  • Custom Apps
  • Analytics
  • Data Science
  • Inventory Management
  • Sales prediction
  • 3rd party integrations

We believe that the success of this application is dependent on building network of clients that can be shown the value of their data and help them fully utilize it while subscribing to our services.
